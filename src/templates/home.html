The Beta-Epsilon chapter of Eta Kappa Nu (HKN) is composed of the top electrical, computer, and computer science engineers at the University of Michigan.

We collabortate with companies to speak at our meetings, as well as, host tech talks for the general campus community.

We serve our community by operating the fully student-run dB Cafe, holding community service projects around Ann Arbor, and orginizing community events to encourage collaboration amoung the students and faculty.
